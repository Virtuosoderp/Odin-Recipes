A SPICY A-MEAT-A-BALL

A delicious meatball (singular) with spaghetti (and booze) inside

Ingredients:

You will need:

  • One 750 ml bottle of smirnoff
  • one metric ton of ground beef
  • two boxed spaghetti (really any noodle you want)
  • one a-ketchup
  • two a-mustard
  • one kraft macaraoni and cheese box
  • nine sticks of butter
  • one Momma-Mia
  • a strong stomach

Steps:

  1. Get out your sauce pan and start boiling water
  2. Put in spaghetti and cook untill limp and lifeless
  3. Gather all of the spaghetti in a big pile
  4. Make a big circle of ground beef in a circular shape and plot spaghetti pile into it
  5. Drench spaghetti in smirnoff between sips of it (you'll need it)
  6. Encase spaghetti in meaty prison
  7. Take out extra large pot
  8. Melt all nine sticks of butter in the pot
  9. Squeeze out all ketchup into pot
  10. Squeeze out all mustard into pot
  11. Take out the cheese packet. Shake out a nice line of orange dust on to the coutner. Open your nose and do a line.
  12. Empty rest of container of cheese packet into ketchup and mustard goop.
  13. Burn the fuck out of it (lol)
  14. Stick meatball spaghetti mess onto a cookie sheet
  15. Drink more smirnoff
  16. Preheat oven to 500 farenheit then bake for 3 hours
  17. Take meatball out of oven and drench in goop.
  18. Mama beat you for ruining her kitchen
  19. Drink to old country
  20. Enjoy spicy a meat-a-ball
  21. Repeat

Congratulations! You have made ONE A-SPICY-SPICY-A-MEAT-A-BALL!
